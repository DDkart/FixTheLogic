What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us make the site work efficiently and provide useful insights into how it is used.

Required for the website to function correctly, including page navigation and security features.

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. All data is aggregated and anonymous.

We display ads via Google AdSense. These may use cookies to serve ads relevant to your browsing interests. These are third-party cookies governed by Google’s privacy policy.

Preference Cookies

These remember your preferences to improve your experience on return visits.

Third-Party Services

Cookies may be set by Google Analytics, Google AdSense, and YouTube (for embedded videos). Please review their privacy policies for full details.

Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking all cookies may affect site functionality. Learn more at allaboutcookies.org.
