Write for Fix The Logic

We are always looking for experienced tech writers, appliance repair enthusiasts, and smart home specialists to contribute high-quality troubleshooting guides to our platform. If you can explain complex procedures in plain, accessible English, we’d love to hear from you.

💡 Tip: Read a few of our published guides before pitching so you have a clear sense of our tone and depth.

What We’re Looking For

We publish original, step-by-step troubleshooting and repair guides for everyday consumers. Topics include:

  • Appliances — washing machines, dishwashers, refrigerators, dryers, ovens
  • Smart Home Devices — Amazon Echo, Google Nest, smart plugs, doorbells
  • Printers & Office Equipment — error codes, paper jams, connectivity issues
  • Software Errors — Windows, macOS, Android, iOS error codes

Our Standards

  • Original content only. No AI-generated or previously published articles.
  • Accurate and tested. Steps must work. Research thoroughly.
  • Plain English. Write for someone attempting a repair for the first time.
  • Full structure. Introduction, steps, summary table, and FAQ required.
  • Images welcome. Original images strongly preferred.

Compensation

We offer a competitive flat fee per accepted article, paid within 7 working days of publication. Contributors receive a full author byline with links to their profiles.

How to Pitch

Send a short pitch via our Contact page using subject line “Write for Us”. Include: article title, 2–3 sentence summary, and 2–3 writing samples. We respond within 3–5 business days.
